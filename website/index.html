What is moonbase?

Moonbase is a social networking platform in the making. It allows users to connect with other users in a unique way. People often come up with great ideas but it is common for that person to not know how to go about it. With moonbase, you can upload your concepts, ideas and projects for the rest of the world to see. Anyone interested in collaborating or working with you can send you a request to be a part of your idea. Along with working, moonbase gives you a great chance to learn new things and grow. To find out more about the backend, visit the GitHub page for moonbase.
